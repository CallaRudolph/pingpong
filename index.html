Ping or Pong? (or both)


Rules of the game:

Enter a number (or don't, but that's not fun) and Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 become 'ping'
  • Numbers divisible by 5 become 'pong'
  • Numbers divisible by 15 become 'ping-pong'
